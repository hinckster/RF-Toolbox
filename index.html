Tool 03 Live

IIP3 / Intermodulation

Polynomial device model (a₁, a₂, a₃) → exact IIP3, IIP2, OIP3, P1dB. Spectrum shows IM2, IM3, 2nd/3rd harmonics. Intercept diagram with gain compression curve.
Expert: derive IIP3 from polynomial coefficients, not just measure it
IIP3 = √(4|a₁|/3|a₃|) · y = a₁x + a₂x² + a₃x³
