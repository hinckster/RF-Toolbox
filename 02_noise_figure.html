Tool 02

Noise Figure Cascade (Friis)

System noise factor and noise figure for a chain of up to 4 stages

F_sys = F₁ + (F₂ − 1)/G₁ + (F₃ − 1)/(G₁·G₂) + (F₄ − 1)/(G₁·G₂·G₃)
NF_sys = 10·log₁₀(F_sys)  |  F = 10^(NF/10)  |  G = 10^(Gain/10)
Each subsequent stage is de-weighted by the cumulative gain preceding it. First stage dominates when G₁ is large.
