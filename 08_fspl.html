FSPL Multi-Band Sweep

Free-space path loss vs distance — up to 4 frequency bands overlaid

FSPL (dB) = 20·log₁₀(4π·d·f / c)
d in metres, f in Hz, c = 3×10⁸ m/s
= 20·log₁₀(d) + 20·log₁₀(f) − 147.55
+6 dB per octave in distance or frequency
