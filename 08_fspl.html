Free-Space Path Loss + Atmospheric Effects

FSPL vs distance for 6 frequency bands simultaneously. Rain attenuation and atmospheric absorption overlays. Band selection guidance.

Free-Space Path Loss (Friis)
FSPL = 20 log₁₀(4πdf/c)
= 20log(d_km) + 20log(f_MHz) + 32.44 dB
= 20log(d_km) + 20log(f_GHz) + 92.44 dB
Valid in far field, LOS, no multipath. Each octave in f = 6 dB more loss.
Rain Attenuation (ITU-R P.838)
A_rain = k · R^α · d [dB]
k, α are frequency-dependent ITU coefficients
R = rain rate [mm/hr], d = path length [km]
Significant above 10 GHz. R = 25 mm/hr = moderate rain. 50 = heavy.
Atmospheric Absorption (ITU-R P.676)
A_atm = (γ_O₂ + γ_H₂O) · d [dB]
O₂ resonance at 60 GHz: ~10 dB/km
H₂O resonance at 22.3 GHz: ~0.2 dB/km
Severe at 60 GHz — limits range to <500 m for most links.
